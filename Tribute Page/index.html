Samuel Harris Altman

The man shaping AI’s future

Early life & education

  • 1985 – Born April 22 in Chicago, Illinois; grew up in St. Louis, Missouri, eldest of four children of a dermatologist mother and real-estate broker father
  • 1993 – At age eight, received his first Apple Macintosh; began coding and tinkering with hardware
  • Attended John Burroughs School, a private prep school in Ladue, Missouri
  • 2003 – Enrolled at Stanford University studying computer science; dropped out in 2005 after two year
  • 2005 – Co‑founded Loopt, a location-based social-networking app, during Stanford days; secured early YC backing, raised over $30 million
  • 2012 – Loopt acquired by Green Dot Corporation for $43 million
  • 2014 – Appointed President of YC, succeeding Paul Graham
  • Under his leadership, YC funded startups like Airbnb, Dropbox, Reddit, Instacart, and DoorDash
  • 2019 – Stepped down as President; became a board chairman to focus on OpenAI .
  • 2015 – Co‑founded OpenAI as a nonprofit with Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, and others
  • 2019 – Became CEO of OpenAI
  • OpenAI board unexpectedly fired him; widespread employee and investor backlash led to his reinstatement days later, with a board rework
