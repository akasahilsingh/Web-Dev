Allo Paratha Recipe

Hey Every one today ill be teaching you how to make aloo paratha recipe.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Boiled aloo
  • oil
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Make dough by mixing flour with water.
  2. Mash boiled aloo and mix spices with it.
  3. Now make round paratha from it.
  4. Now garnish it with fresh dahi.
